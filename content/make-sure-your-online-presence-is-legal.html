Make sure your online presence is legal

 Your online communications need to comply with laws of other countries. If you operate in heavily regulated sectors such as alcohol, gambling or financial services, this is particularly important.

In many cases, research and applying social media best practice should be sufficient to keep you within the law. However if you’re in doubt, consult with a legal expert, who can help you adapt your systems and processes to minimise the risks.

DIT has a regional network of e-commerce advisers who can advise you on reaching global customers online – contact your local office.

And the Department for International Trade’s E-Exporting programme helps UK retailers and brands accelerate their global growth through e-commerce. Visit gov.uk/e-exporting to find out more.
