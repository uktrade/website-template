Get ready to manage regulations, legal issues and risk

You need to be aware of any regulations that are relevant to your product or service, any legislation or legal issues that apply and any documentation you need. Be prepared to research this thoroughly before you export and to take professional advice if necessary.

To find out about the regulations and legal requirements of the market you’re exporting to, see the Department for International Trade’s exporting country guides.

See our guidance on simplifying regulations, including licences, customs and sanctions.

You should also take any necessary steps to protect your intellectual property. Read guidance on exporting your intellectual property on GOV.UK.
