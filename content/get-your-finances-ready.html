Get your finances ready

You may need extra financial resources to develop overseas markets, and the flexibility and capital to scale up if export orders are coming in quickly. Don’t forget to factor any overseas travel, marketing and product modification into your budget.

You’ll want to have staff and processes in place to deal with:

  • export enquiries
  • international sales
  • managing overseas contracts
  • getting paid

UK Export Finance (UKEF) may be able to help. The UK’s export credit agency, UKEF helps UK companies of all sizes and in all sectors to win, fulfill and get paid for export contracts.

See our guidance on finance for export.
