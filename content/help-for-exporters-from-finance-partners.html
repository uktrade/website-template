Banking and accounting services

Banking services

The Department for International Trade partners with high street banks that offer a growing range of services and support to help you export.

The government is working with these banks to offer integrated support and services for the full range of your exporting requirements, from finding new buyers through to getting paid and growing the business.

And UK Export Finance works with these banks to provide finance for exporters.

See how they compare through an independent survey of the UK’s business banking accounts, credit cards, loans and other services, which asks thousands of small and medium-sized business owners about their experiences.

Accounting services

You can find an accountant through accountancy bodies such as the ICAEW or ACCA (listed below). The government also publishes a list of accounting software that has been tested by Companies House. Some of these are available from our partners, along with advice and additional services.
